# Trend-Based Early Warnings

## User Story

As a planner, I want trend-based early warnings before hard triggers trip the system.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Early warning logic detects deteriorating trends before hard circuit-breaker triggers.
- [ ] Warning signals are clearly differentiated from hard-stop triggers.
- [ ] Warnings are visible to operators and planners through existing reporting surfaces.
- [ ] Early warnings remain explainable with observable metric inputs.
