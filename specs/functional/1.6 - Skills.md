# Skills

Domain-specific protocols that specialize agent behavior inside the contract.

## Description

Skills are modular `SKILL.md` playbooks loaded when a task enters a known domain (debugging, testing, review, architecture, spec work, etc.) or when explicitly requested by the user.

The contract remains the governing layer. Skills do not override gates or invariants; they provide domain methodology, checks, anti-patterns, and escalation paths.

## Features

- 1.6.1 — [Debugging](../../skills/debugging/SKILL.md) — Mandatory debugging protocol with hypothesis discipline
- 1.6.2 — [Testing](../../skills/testing/SKILL.md) — Test design, validation, and oracle quality rules
- 1.6.3 — [Code Review](../../skills/code-review/SKILL.md) — P0-P3 review methodology and risk prioritization
- 1.6.4 — [Software Architecture Review](../../skills/software-architecture-review/SKILL.md) — Structural coherence and architecture trade-off checks
- 1.6.5 — [Clean Code](../../skills/clean-code/SKILL.md) — Pre-commit refactoring and hygiene pass
- 1.6.6 — [Spec Review](../../skills/spec-review/SKILL.md) — Specification quality and consistency review
- 1.6.7 — [Generic Subagent](../../skills/generic-subagent/SKILL.md) — Read-only delegation protocol and thresholds
- 1.6.8 — [Systemic Thinking](../../skills/systemic-thinking/SKILL.md) — System-level coherence and risk analysis
- 1.6.9 — [ADR Backfill](../../skills/adr-backfill/SKILL.md) — Recover architecture decisions from history
- 1.6.10 — [Spec Backfill](../../skills/spec-backfill/SKILL.md) — Reconstruct feature-level specs from implementation
- 1.6.11 — [Code-Spec Backfill](../../skills/code-spec-backfill/SKILL.md) — Backfill function-level contracts
- 1.6.12 — [Black-Box Red Testing](../../skills/black-box-red-testing/SKILL.md) — Failing tests that reveal real defects
- 1.6.13 — [White-Box Red Testing](../../skills/white-box-red-testing/SKILL.md) — Targeted adversarial tests from implementation analysis

- 1.6.15 — [Have You Considered](../../skills/have-you-considered/SKILL.md) — Structured alternative exploration
- 1.6.16 — [Feynman](../../skills/feynman/SKILL.md) — Explain complex topics in simplified form

## Boundaries

**In scope:**
- Domain procedures and triggers
- Skill-specific anti-patterns and quality bars
- Subagent delegation and escalation criteria

**Out of scope:**
- Contract authority and gate semantics (see 1.1 Contract System)
- Blackboard/task lifecycle mechanics (see 1.2 and 1.3)
